Emergency Support

Our app uses advanced GPS technology to locate healthcare providers near you, no matter where you are. This feature is especially crucial during emergencies when every second counts.

Symptoms & Triage

Engage privately with our AI-powered CareRequest, Summarizer, or Diagnoser to gain insights into the cause of your symptoms and explore potential treatment options. You can share the generated report with healthcare professionals for second opinions and added peace of mind.

Health Monitoring

We provide several tools for capturing, analysing, and monitoring health data, including blood pressure, diabetes, weight, temperature, episodes, and other tests. This includes setting up thresholds that will automatically send messages to healthcare providers and family members.

Supporting Citizens with
low Digital Literacy

We provide comprehensive training for Community Digital Health Assistants (CDHA) to enable them to offer citizens, who may not own or know how to use smartphones, the opportunity to benefit from our App using the Community Edition of the app, thereby promoting health equality."

Medication Administration

Sets the correct dose, frequency, and reminders for medications. Provides reports on missed doses, ensuring you stay on track with your treatment plan and receive timely alerts.

Remote Care Monitoring

Provides automatic alerts to carers and relatives about the health status of their loved ones. This system ensures timely notifications and offers the option to facilitate support when needed, enhancing the overall care experience and ensuring peace of mind for families.

Appointment Management

Enables booking and monitoring appointments with healthcare providers, including alerts and capturing appointment outcomes.

Health Information

Uses a trained AI medical model to answer myriad health questions, providing a single source of medical truth in confidence.

Access Control Governance

You can authorize your care provider or any medical consultant anywhere in the world to view your health records and understand your health journey before prescribing treatment. This access can be granted for a fixed duration and extended if required. You have the option to select what information they can see, and you can revoke access at any point in time.
