Empowering Global Health Equity Through Digital Innovation

HealthCentreApp is a UK-based startup shaped by over 12 years of dedicated research and 30+ years of expertise in Electronic Patient Records (EPR) innovation, led by former senior clinical and digital managers from the UK NHS.

Our foundation is strengthened by advanced knowledge from prestigious institutions, including AI in Health education at MIT, Global Health studies at Harvard University, AI Ethics at LSE, and Data Science in Healthcare training at the Geisel School of Medicine.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge health inequalities through modern technology, making healthcare accessible, efficient, and equitable for all. Our commitment ensures that everyone receives quality care at their point of need, regardless of location or socioeconomic status.

Our Solution

We've developed a cutting-edge EHR system that seamlessly integrates various features to provide comprehensive healthcare solutions.

Personal Mobile Health Record System

Access to secure, health records anytime, anywhere.

Emergency Healthcare Access

Instant medical support when needed.

AI Symptom Checker

Enhancing diagnostics, predictive analytics, and decision-making.

CareConnect

Connecting patients with consultants and family worldwide.

Remote Care

Using threshold alerts to notify caregivers and family members.

Health Monitoring

Enabling early interventions, reducing costs, and improving health outcomes, particularly in developing countries.

Trained Local Digital Health Practitioners

Empowering individuals to support deprived community members.

Advanced Data Governance

Giving patients control over their health data and access permissions.

By merging AI and digital technology with human-centered healthcare, we ensure seamless medical record management, improved healthcare access, and sustainable income opportunities for trained community members.
